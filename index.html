Lets Understand Glaucoma

What does Glaucoma entail?

Glaucoma comprises a set of eye conditions that harm the optic nerve, usually resulting from elevated pressure within the eye.

Identifying Glaucoma

Signs may encompass vision blurriness, intense eye pain, headaches, and the perception of halos around lights.

Caring for Those with Glaucoma

Routine eye examinations, medications as prescribed, and lifestyle modifications can effectively control glaucoma.
